Our Story

Built on a
Foundation of Trust

KMS Power Solutions was founded in 2012 by engineers who saw a critical gap in Uganda's power equipment market — businesses were struggling to find reliable suppliers who combined quality products with genuine after-sales support.

Starting from a small workshop in Industrial Area, Kampala, we grew our reputation one client at a time. Word spread that we didn't just sell equipment — we stood behind every unit we delivered and every service we performed.

Today, we are the preferred power solutions partner for hospitals, hotels, manufacturing plants, telecom companies, and construction firms across Uganda and the wider East African region.
